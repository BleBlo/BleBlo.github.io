PRISM: Revolutionary AI-Powered Investment Tool

PDF Retrieval, Interaction, and Smart Management

During my internship at the Abu Dhabi Investment Authority in summer 2024, I spearheaded the development of PRISM, an artificial intelligence-powered tool that fundamentally transformed how investment data is processed across the organization.

The Challenge

Investment analysts faced a critical bottleneck: manually extracting and analyzing data from over 200 sources took approximately 25.5 hours per quarterly cycle. This time-consuming process limited the speed of decision-making and introduced potential for human error in financial analysis.

Technical Implementation

PRISM represents a comprehensive solution integrating multiple advanced technologies to automate complex data analysis workflows.

Core Technologies
Python, Streamlit, PyMuPDF, Advanced API Integrations
Processing Speed
Reduced 25.5 hours to 2 minutes per document
Accuracy Rate
Exceeded 98% accuracy, surpassing manual methods
Time Reduction
Achieved 96% reduction in extraction time
Department Impact
Successfully deployed across six major departments
Data Sources
Processes information from 200+ investment sources

Key Achievements

  • Efficiency Revolution - Transformed a 25.5-hour quarterly process into streamlined 2-minute operations
  • Cross-Department Adoption - Created versatile tool with applications across six major departments
  • Superior Accuracy - Exceeded 98% accuracy rate, outperforming manual extraction methods
  • Scalable Architecture - Positioned for future enhancements in file management and workflow optimization

Impact and Recognition

PRISM demonstrates how strategic application of artificial intelligence can revolutionize traditional financial processes, creating measurable value through automation and accuracy improvements.
